### Cost functions

- cost function in machine learning is a formula that measures the "cost" or **error** of a model. 

- The main goal during model training is to find the parameters (weights and biases) that minimize this cost function.

- The most common cost function for regression is the *Mean Squared Error (MSE)*. However, for classification, you can't use MSE because the prediction is a probability (between 0 and 1). Instead, the most common cost function is Log Loss, also known as **Binary Cross-Entropy**.
